Party at the Beach!

Join us for an unforgettable evening of fun, food, and frolic at the iconic Amanzimtoti Beach with the Amanzimtoti Chamber of Commerce's Sunset Soiree. As the sun dips below the horizon, painting the sky with hues of orange and pink, Amanzimtoti Beach transforms into a vibrant party venue, alive with the spirit of community and camaraderie. Revel in an array of beach games designed for all agesâ€”from sandcastle competitions to beach volleyball tournaments, ensuring laughter and lively competition. Savor the taste of summer with gourmet food trucks lining the shore, offering a variety of local and international delicacies, alongside refreshing cocktails and mocktails. As the evening progresses, gather around the bonfire for storytelling, live music, and dance under the stars. The Sunset Soiree promises a night of unforgettable memories, fostering community bonds and celebrating the beauty of our coastal gem.

Ice Skate at the Mall

Experience the magic of winter in the heart of our city at this glacial gala, hosted by the Amanzimtoti Chamber of Commerce at the Galleria Mall. This exclusive event invites you and your loved ones to glide across the ice at a specially discounted rate, turning an ordinary day into an extraordinary adventure. The ice rink, set against a backdrop of twinkling lights and festive decorations, offers a winter wonderland escape, complete with the melodies of seasonal favorites to enhance your skating experience. Whether you're a novice or a seasoned skater, Galleria welcomes all to enjoy the thrill of ice skating. Off the rink, indulge in winter treats like hot cocoa, gourmet marshmallows, and artisanal pastries from local vendors. Interactive workshops and performances by professional skaters will dazzle and inspire, making the Glacial Gala at Galleria Mall a cherished event that combines the joy of winter sports with the warmth of community spirit.
