Membership Benefits:

NP Membership: No fee

  • Access to exclusive networking events tailored for non-profit organizations.
  • Free or discounted participation in workshops and seminars focused on nonprofit management and fundraising.
  • Listing in our directory of non-profit organizations, enhancing visibility and credibility within the community.
  • Opportunity to apply for grants and funding opportunities exclusively available to non-profit members.

    • Bronze Membership: Basic benefits

  • Access to monthly webinars covering various topics relevant to small businesses.
  • Featured spotlight position on our website for one month, increasing visibility and brand recognition.
  • Discounted rates for advertising and promotional opportunities on our platform.
  • Priority registration and discounts for conferences and industry events.

    • Silver Membership: Enhanced benefits

  • Access to premium industry reports and research materials.
  • Quarterly networking luncheons with industry experts and peers.
  • Featured placement in our monthly newsletter reaching thousands of subscribers.
  • Enhanced visibility with a dedicated profile page on our website.

    • Gold Membership: Premium benefits

  • VIP access to exclusive events and workshops featuring renowned speakers and industry leaders.
  • Personalized consultation sessions with our team of experts to address specific business needs.
  • Priority consideration for sponsorships and partnership opportunities with our organization.
  • Customized advertising packages tailored to maximize brand exposure and engagement.
