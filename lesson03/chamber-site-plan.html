Amanzimtoti Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

By Austin Barnes

Purpose

As Chamber of Commerce in Amanzimtoti, South Africa we are charged with holding events that help grow our town. This includes but is not limited to conferences, events, festivals, and more. Using these we help grow all our businesses and our town.

Scenarios

Below are two personas that may access the website with questions in mind.

Tholulwazi

A local who wants to get involved with the community.

He may come to the website to get answers for the questions:
What events are happening in Amanzimtoti soon?
How can I participate with the chamber and in events?

Amanda

A local business owner wanting to learn about the chamber of commerce.

She may come to the website to get answers for the questions:
What companies are a part of the chamber?
How have these events helped the city?
